D21S210: a highly polymorphic (GT)n marker closely linked to the beta-amyloid
protein precursor (APP) gene.

Warren AC(1), McInnis MG, Kalaitsidaki M, Cox TK, Blaschak J, Chakravarti A,
Antonarakis SE.

Author information: 
(1)Center for Medical Genetics, Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine,
Baltimore, MD 21205.

We describe a highly polymorphic (GT)n repeat with 14 alleles that is closely
linked to the amyloid precursor protein (APP) gene on human chromosome 21. This
marker, D21S210, will be useful for studies of linkage of disorders such as
Alzheimer disease to the APP gene.
